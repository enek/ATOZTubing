About

ATOZ Tubing Services Ltd is 100% Canadian owned and operated serving the Canadian Oilfield since 2013 operating out of Edmonton, Grande Prairie.

The primary role of Atoz Tubing Services is to ensure the invested Tubular interests of Oil Companies are independently represented. Our field representatives are trained in acceptance of product, transportation, field assembly and storage. ATOZ will ensure all quality control facets from the mill to assembly of your product at the well site are accomplished safely and cost effectively. Enhanced quality control procedures and global networking enables us to provide our customers with superior products, superior services, and measurable cost savings.

Safety

ATOZ Tubing Services is committed to providing a safe work environment for its employees, clients and other work personnel present at the work site. As a SECOR regulated and approved company, ATOZ tubing Services is compliant with all safety programs, including ComplyWorks, and ISNetworld in addition to our custom in-house programs and in field.

We take our commitment to safety very seriously. We honour and respect the need for laws and regulations in the workplace and have worked hard to develop a comprehensive safety program.

We maintain adequate standards of maintenance on our handling equipment and trucks ensuring all hazards are guarded or eliminated.

Our Safety Commitment

Our goal is to have an injury and accident-free workplace. We strive to maintain a safe and healthy workplace and provide safety equipment, training and the time to make this possible.
